Day03
Charleston → Savannah, GA
~2 hours driving • ~110 miles

Another relaxed drive south on I-95. Savannah is famous for its moss-draped squares, ghost tours, and vibrant City Market. Spend the afternoon wandering on foot.

  • 🚗
    Depart Charleston
    Take US-17 S for scenic coastal views, or I-95 S for speed
    10:00 AM
  • 🎨
    Savannah City Market
    Art galleries, restaurants, live music — open until 10 PM
    ~12:00 PM
  • 🌳
    Forsyth Park & Historic Squares
    22 moss-draped squares — a must-see on foot
    Afternoon
  • 👻
    Evening Ghost Tour
    Savannah is one of America's most haunted cities!
    Evening

Be Prepared
Road Trip Tips
⛽
Gas Up Smart
Use GasBuddy app to find cheapest gas on route. Fill up before entering Georgia — prices vary.
🏨
Book Hotels Early
Charleston & Savannah fill up fast. Book on Booking.com or Hotels.com at least 2 weeks ahead.
🌡️
Weather Check
The South can be hot and humid. Bring light clothes, sunscreen, and a rain jacket just in case.
🗺️
Download Maps
Download offline Google Maps for all cities before you leave — cell signal can be spotty in rural areas.
🅿️
Parking in Cities
Use SpotHero or ParkWhiz apps to pre-book parking in Charleston and Savannah. Street parking is limited.
🍽️
Food Reservations
Make dinner reservations in advance for Charleston — popular spots like Husk book up days ahead.
